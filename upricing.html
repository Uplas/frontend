Pricing FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards (via Stripe and Flutterwave), PayPal, M-Pesa for users in supported regions, and direct bank transfers for larger plans or enterprise solutions.

Is there a free trial?

While we don't offer a traditional time-limited free trial for full plans, some introductory modules or lessons within courses may be available for free preview. You can also explore our free resources on the blog and community forums to get a feel for Uplas.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, for monthly and yearly subscriptions, you can cancel anytime. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 30-day risk-free guarantee on our yearly Full Platform Access plan.
