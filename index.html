It's a valid concern! While AI automates some tasks, it primarily *changes* jobs, creating new roles and demanding new skills. AI excels at processing data, but lacks human creativity, critical thinking, and empathy. The key isn't fearing replacement, but learning to collaborate with AI ‚Äì letting it handle the repetitive work so you can focus on higher-value tasks. Uplas teaches you how.
